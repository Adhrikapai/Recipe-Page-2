TOMATO RICE

AUTHENTIC ASIAN TOMATO RICE

Health Benefits of Tomatoes: Reduced risk of heart disease, Supports bone health, Anti Cancer benefits, Reduced risk of neurological diseases and Obesity.

    INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 Cup Cooked White Rice
  • 1 big size Tomato
  • 1 big size Onion
  • 1 Tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cloves of Garlic
  • 1 inch piece of Ginger
  • 1 Tbsp Red Chilli powder
  • 1/4 piece of an onion
  • 3 Tbsp Freshly grated Coconut
  • 1 Tbsp Coriander seeds
  • 1 small piece of Cinnamon stick
  • 3 buds of a Star Anise

PREPARATION:

First, grind the garlic, ginger, star anise, cinnamon stick, coriander seeds, freshly grated coconut, onion and red chilli powder into a smooth paste. Second, heat the oil in a pan and add the chopped onions, fry till they go translucent and add the paste we made already, fry till the raw smell goes away. Third, add the chopped tomatoes and a little salt, fry till they are cooked well, later on add the cooked rice and keep mixing till the masala coats the rice.
